# WunderlustDMV

Welcome to **WunderlustDMV**, a bureaucratic underworld simulation game built in Unity. 

Take on the role of a lowly employee working at the infernal DMV. Process a never-ending line of frustrated customers, manage your energy (Will) and corporate standing (Performance), and try to survive the shift without getting fired—or worse, performing so well that you get reincarnated!

## 📋 Features

- **Paperwork Processing System**: Accurately accept or reject paperwork from a queue of unique customers. Watch out for their rising Frustration!
- **Corporate Survival Mechanics**: Balance your *Will* (energy), *Performance*, and *Attention*. Run out of Will and you're done; draw too much Attention and management will make your life miserable.
- **Underworld Politics**: Gain reputation with demonic entities like Astaroth, Verrine, and Sothoth to trigger special events and side-quests.
- **Boss "Battles"**: Face off against middle-management "Nepo-babies" in intense performance reviews. 
- **Meta-Progression**: Spend your hard-earned *Office Obols* at the Vending Machine for combat buffs, and use *Chthonic Credits* to upgrade your apartment with new furniture and professional certifications.
- **Dynamic Run Paths**: Choose your daily schedule (Vending Machine, Break Room, Performance Reviews) using the in-game calendar system.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Engine:** Unity 6
- **Language:** C#
- **UI:** TextMeshPro & Unity UI 

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start your Shift:** Face a queue of chaotic, underworld customers.
2. **Take Actions:** Use your Action loadout to calm customers, process paperwork, or escalate issues. Every action costs *Will* and impacts your overall *Performance*.
3. **Survive the Week:** Earn enough currency to pay your landlord and buy supplies. 
4. **Don't Overachieve:** Poor performance leads to termination, but flying too close to the sun results in forced reincarnation. Find the perfect mediocre balance!
